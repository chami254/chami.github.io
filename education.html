Education

Global One works with girls and women of all ages recognizing that often the impact of dis-empowerment affects girls from the moment they are born. It is therefore critical to ensure that education for girls as well as their families is prioritized to ensure that communities have the knowledge to empower their girls and women.

Orphan Sponsorship

Global one Kenya is currently sponsoring 90 orphans; as follows:



  • a) 10 home based in Korogocho informal settlement.
  • b) 80 orphans at the Young Muslim Centre under institutional based program both in primary and high school.


These students are spread in 45 primary schools and 36 high schools and come from diverse social and humble backgrounds from all over Kenya.

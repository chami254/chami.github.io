Global One Kenya

Global One Kenya (GO-K) is a dynamic foward thinking organisation with its headquarters in the UK. The organisation is established and governed by a group of self-driven Muslim women empowered by the trust that a better world for all humanity is possible.GO-K was founded in 2012 based on the belief that there is a need and possibility for more women to come foward as change-makers in positions of leadership amongst the communities.


-Our Work-





Food Security and Climate Change



Under this thematic area we address agriculture and climate change to improve food security for women/girls by encouraging farmers to adapt organic farming practices using climate smart technologies.



See More >


Health



GO-K focusses on reproductive health addressing issues related to maternal and child health with the objective to reduce maternal and child mortality and morbidity as well as eradicate all forms of gender based violence.



See More >


Wash



GO-K has worked with communities and other stakeholders in rural areas and informal settlements of Nairobi Kenya to deliver WASH projects to address issues of water borne diseases and lack of menstrual hygiene management.



See More >


Livelihood



GO-K has been working with under-served and vulnerable widows and orphans by helping them alleviate their burden by supporting them in setting up income generating activities so as to diversify their sources of income thus improve their standards of living.
